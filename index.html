FAQ

Frequntly Asked Question

What are your office hours?

Our office hours vary by location, but typically we are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Some locations may offer extended hours or weekend appointments. Please contact your nearest clinic for specific hours.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by visiting our website and using our online booking system. Alternatively, you can call our office at [phone number] during business hours to speak with our scheduling team.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact us or check with your insurance provider to verify coverage and any out-of-pocket costs.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance card(s), photo ID (such as driver's license or passport), list of current medications, any relevant medical records or test results, and co-payment or deductible payment if applicable to your appointment.

Do you offer telemedicine appointments?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for certain types of visits. To schedule a telemedicine appointment, please mention your preference when scheduling, or inquire about telemedicine options during your call.
